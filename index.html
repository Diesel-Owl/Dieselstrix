My Story & Mission

For the last 12 years, I worked in law enforcement. The job required discipline and responsibility, but over time I realized I had stopped doing things for myself. I was surviving, not living.

DieselStrix is my turning point. This project is about building something with my own hands that reminds me — and hopefully others — that your dreams don't expire because life got heavy.

What is DieselStrix?

DieselStrix is a custom STOL backcountry aircraft I am building myself — not purchased, built. It combines:

  • CH-750 cabin & main wings (short takeoff performance)
  • CH-650 tail (clean cruise stability)
  • A Cummins R2.8 diesel engine for reliability and torque

We are allowed to build our lives differently.
