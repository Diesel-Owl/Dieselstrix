The Journey

For the last 12 years, I’ve worked in law enforcement. It’s a job that requires discipline, responsibility, and a steady mind. But somewhere along the way, I realized I had stopped doing things for myself. I was surviving, not living.

DieselStrix is my turning point.

This project is about building something with my own hands that reminds me — and hopefully others — that your dreams don’t expire just because life got heavy.

What is DieselStrix?

DieselStrix is a custom STOL backcountry aircraft that I am building myself. Not purchased. Built.

It’s a hybrid design using:

  • CH-750 cabin and main wings for rugged short takeoff capability
  • CH-650 tail for clean control and stability in cruise
  • Powered by a Cummins diesel engine for reliability and efficiency

This plane isn’t just a machine. It’s a statement:

We are allowed to build our lives differently.

Why I’m Asking for Support

I’m working to earn my Private Pilot License and build DieselStrix from the ground up. Every step of this journey will be documented — the training, the build progress, the challenges, and the breakthroughs.

The goal isn’t just to fly.

The goal is to show others that you can start your dream from exactly where you are.

No perfect timing. No waiting for later. Just one step at a time.

What the Funds Will Help With

  • Flight training & PPL certification
  • Airframe materials and build components
  • Safety inspections & FAA compliance
  • Documenting the journey so others can follow and learn
